# GUIDELINES.md — VAULT-FAST Quality Rules

## Source of Truth Rules
- Draft from source material, not memory.
- Preserve raw provenance in `sources`.
- Keep mutable work in Inbox/drafts; canonical finalization belongs to VAULT.

## Quality Bar
A VAULT-FAST batch is good when:
- drafts are schema-valid
- provenance is explicit
- ambiguity is clearly flagged
- handoff packets are complete
- Inbox trend improves without quality regression
